Did you know that everything we have is a gift from god, and actually belongs to God? The word of God teaches us that many things are a gift from god. Our life, our freedom, our intellect, our ability to worship him - and even our children - or our future children!

That's right. Even the money we earn belongs to him, and we should use it to bring Glory to him. Money is a complicated issue. Sometimes we aren't sure how we should save it, sometimes we aren't sure how we should spend it, and sometimes it becomes a problem for us!

Here are a couple useful verses about money...

  • Prov 13:11 - "Dishonest money dwindles away, but whoever gathers money little by little makes it grow.
  • Prov 3:9 - "Honor the LORD with your wealth, with the firstfruits of your crops.
